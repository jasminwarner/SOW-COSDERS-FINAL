My name is Jasmin and this is the website I created focusing on a way to help the lack of creative spaces for youth, like myself, who love Visual Arts. I am very passionate about Visual Arts, mainly Film and Photography, and got frustrated of there not being enough opportunities for me to showcase and learn more about my craft. Even when there were available opportunities, they came with a very big price tag so I was not able to participate. I beleive this solution would be one of the best ways to deal with my social issue because it gives youth a free space to express themselves.
