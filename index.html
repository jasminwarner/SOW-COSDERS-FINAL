What We Do

At Inspire Visuals we provide a free creative center for youth in Barbados to develop their passions in Visual Arts.
Artforms such as:

  • Filmmaking
  • Design
  • Photography
  • Painting
  • Drawing
  • Crafts

How We do It

How This Helps

By having a free center, youth can create and learn different artforms with their peers, not having to worry about paying to do something they love.This gives them a healthy stress releiver and a way to express themselves. It also makes them a more well rounded person in society as the career field of Visual Arts is very diverse.
